Hello! I am a Master's student in Computer Science (AI) at Sogang University, working in the Bigdata Processing & DB Lab. I am also participating in the 2025 AI Convergence Education Program at the University of Toronto, sponsored by the Korean Government (IITP), where I collaborate with the LG Toronto AI Lab.

Previously, I graduated from Konkuk University with a degree in Management of Technology and Real Estate, and worked at Hyundai Department Store Group in the Marketing Team. I also completed the POSCO AI·Big Data program at POSTECH.

My current research topics are:

  1. Multi-Agent Systems and evaluation frameworks for Agentic AI
  2. Knowledge Graph–based Retrieval-Augmented Generation (KG-RAG)
  3. Video Anomaly Detection with spatio-temporal efficiency
